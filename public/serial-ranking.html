The Best Serials According to Juliya

My top 3 serials of all-time

Only for Love

Is a romantic Chinese drama about a young journalist, Zheng Shunyuan, who is determined
to build her career and land an important interview. She meets Shi Yan, a successful but cold
businessman. Their relationship begins as a professional, mutually beneficial arrangement,
but slowly grows into genuine feelings.
The series mixes romance, office drama, humor, and touching moments of building trust.

Endless Love

Is a Turkish romantic drama about Nihan, a wealthy young woman, and Kemal, a humble
engineer. Despite coming from completely different worlds, they fall deeply in love. But Nihan
is forced into a marriage with a powerful and dangerous man to protect her family. Years later,
Kemal returns, determined to uncover the truth and fight for their love.
The series is full of passion, secrets, betrayal, and dramatic twists â€” a classic intense Turkish romance.

Healer

Is a Korean action-romance drama about a mysterious night courier known as Healer,
whose job is to complete dangerous missions with absolute secrecy. When he meets a brave young journalist,
Chae Young-shin, their lives intertwine as they uncover a decades-old conspiracy
connecting their pasts. Between action, hacking, undercover identities and emotional depth, the story grows
into a powerful romance.
